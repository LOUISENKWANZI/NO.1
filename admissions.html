Admission Process

  1. Complete the application form (available online or at school office)
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school reports)
  3. Attend an interview (for some classes)
  4. Receive admission letter
  5. Pay required fees

Admission Requirements

Form 1 (S1) Entry

  • Completed Primary Education
  • Primary Leaving Examination Certificate
  • Good conduct from previous school
